Oviraptor

The Misunderstood Dinosaur
Discover the Oviraptor

Meet Oviraptor, whose name means "egg thief," though recent discoveries reveal this dinosaur was actually a devoted parent protecting its own eggs. Living during the Late Cretaceous period, approximately 75 million years ago, Oviraptor was a bird-like dinosaur with a distinctive crest and beak. Through augmented reality, explore its unique features, nesting behaviors, and the fascinating story of how paleontologists discovered the truth about this misunderstood dinosaur's caring nature.
